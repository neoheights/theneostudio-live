Industrial & Factory
Interiors

Functional. Durable. Scalable. Designing for the heart of industry.

Industrial interiors require a unique blend of ruggedness and refined aesthetics. At Neo Studio, we specialize in factory-integrated offices, warehouse showroom units, and large-scale industrial facility designs across Bangalore and South India. Our goal is to create environments that facilitate productivity while reflecting your company's industrial strength.

Principles of Industrial Design

When designing for industrial settings, we prioritize structural integrity and workflow optimization. This includes raw materials like exposed steel, polished concrete, and durable wood that can withstand the demands of a high-activity environment.

Our designs are not just about looks—they are about the smart use of space for equipment, storage, and office administration, ensuring every square foot serves a functional purpose.

Industrial Specializations

Factory Showrooms

Designing spaces where clients can see your manufacturing process in action alongside a polished product gallery.

Industrial Lofts & Mezzanines

Safety-First Interiors

Strategic Integration

The Neo Studio brings years of experience in working with architectural firms and civil engineers to deliver industrial interiors seamlessly integrated with the primary manufacturing facility.

Our team understands the technical requirements of industrial environments, from load-bearing floors to specialized ventilation and lighting systems.
